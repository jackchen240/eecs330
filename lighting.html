Energy Saving Tips

  • LED lightbulbs use 25% as much energy as traditional incandescent bulbs. Switching to LEDs can save lots of electricity!
  • It looks like you use lots of lighting during the day. Try turning off the lights and using natural lighting to save!
  • You use more electricity on lighting than your neighbors. Try using lamps if you don't need to light up the whole room.
